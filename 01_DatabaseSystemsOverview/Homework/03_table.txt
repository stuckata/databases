What is “Table” in Database Terms
=================================
A table is a collection of related data entries and it consists of columns and rows. 

How information is stored in tables?
====================================
Tables contain records (rows) with data.

What is the difference between tables and relations?
====================================================
Each table is a metaphorical representation of an entity or object that is in a tabular format consisting of columns and rows. Columns are the fields of a record or the attributes of an entity. The rows contain the values or data instances. These are also called records or tuples.
Relations exist both among the columns within a table and among the tables.
